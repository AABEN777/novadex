How It Works

Swap in 3 Steps.

No sign-up, no KYC, no waiting. Just connect and swap.

01
Add Arc Testnet
Add Arc Testnet to your EVM wallet — MetaMask, Rabby, or Zerion. NovaDEX will auto-prompt the network switch.
02

Claim free USDC and EURC from the Circle Faucet. You'll need USDC for gas fees and tokens to swap.
